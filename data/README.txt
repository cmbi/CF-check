To get the data:

1) download PDBFIND2.TXT from ftp://ftp.cmbi.umcn.nl/pub/molbio/data/pdbfinder2/
2) run `bash PDBconvert.sh` in linux terminal. Alternatively delete lines 1-44 and the last line of PDBFIND2.TXT, and rename it to PDBfinder2.txt 
3) If wanted.txt is not present or corrupted, scrape it from:  https://swift.cmbi.umcn.nl/gv/select/index.html
